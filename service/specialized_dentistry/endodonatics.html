Endodontics

Endodontics is a vital specialty in dentistry focused • on treatment of dental caries, defects, and diseases of the dental pulp and periapical tissues. It emphasizes saving the tooth structure and maintaining function and aesthetics. It also restores decayed, fractured, or worn teeth.

Why Visit a General Dentist?
General Dentists are responsible for your primary Diagnosis and Evaluation, providing Preventive Care like teeth cleaning, sealants, education oral hygiene and performing Basic Restorative Procedures such as fillings, crowns, and bridges for missing teeth.
