General Dentistry

  • General Dentistry plays a foundational and integrative role. While the hospital may house various dental specialists (like orthodontists, periodontists, oral surgeons, etc.), general dentists are often the first point of contact for patients and serve as coordinators of overall oral care.

Why Visit a General Dentist?
General Dentists are responsible for your primary Diagnosis and Evaluation, providing Preventive Care like teeth cleaning, sealants, education oral hygiene and performing Basic Restorative Procedures such as fillings, crowns, and bridges for missing teeth.

General Dentist work closely with specialists in multidisciplinary cases such as full-mouth rehabilitation or cancer treatment. They use oral tests, x-rays, and other means to evaluate and diagnose your oral problem before prescribing treatment or referring to a specialist.

In short, general dentistry serves as the backbone of a dental specialty hospital, ensuring patients receive timely, effective, and well-coordinated care.
