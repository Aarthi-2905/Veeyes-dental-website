Gum Care & Treatment

  • Periodontics is the dental specialty focused on the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of diseases affecting the gums and supporting structures of the teeth, including the periodontal ligament, alveolar bone, and gingiva.
  • It also involves the placement and maintenance of dental implants and addresses aesthetic concerns related to the gums.

Periodontal health is closely linked to overall systemic health, particularly conditions like:

  • Diabetes
  • Cardiovascular disease
  • Pregnancy complications

“Untreated periodontal disease can lead to tooth loss”
