Accident & Emergency Care

(Maxillofacial & Dental)

  • Accident & Emergency Care focuses on providing quick, treatment for injuries and trauma that can affect your appearance, oral function, and overall health.
  • At VEEYES Dental Hospital (the first NABH accredited dental hospital in Coimbatore), we are equipped with advanced technology and a skilled specialist team to manage complex trauma cases, ensuring optimal care and swift recovery.

Key Features of Maxillofacial & Dental Emergency Care at VEEYES Dental Hospital

  • 24/7 Emergency Services: We are always available to handle emergencies, providing round-the-clock care
  • Advanced Diagnostic Tools: Digital X-rays, CBCT (Cone Beam CT), and intraoral cameras for precise evaluation and diagnosis.
  • In-House Surgical Facilities: Fully equipped operating theatres and a surgical ICU for critical cases, ensuring seamless care.
  • Expert Team: Our team is led by highly experienced oral and maxillofacial surgeons, endodontists, and prosthodontists who specialize in emergency care.
  • Multidisciplinary Approach: We collaborate with plastic surgeons, ENT specialists, and anesthetists for comprehensive treatment of complex trauma cases.
  • Prevents Complications: Immediate care helps reduce the risk of infection and further complications.

Scope of Accident & Emergency Services

Emergency Dental Care
  • Avulsed (Knocked-Out) Teeth: Immediate reimplantation or replacement to restore tooth function and aesthetics.
  • Dental Fractures: Quick restoration using bonding, crowns, or veneers to fix broken or cracked teeth.
  • Severe Toothache or Infection: Immediate relief through root canal treatments or abscess drainage.
Maxillofacial Trauma

Road Traffic Accidents (RTA): Comprehensive care for fractures, dislocations, and soft tissue injuries caused by accidents.

  • Fractures of the Jaw (Mandible and Maxilla): We perform open reduction and internal fixation (ORIF) using titanium plates and screws to restore jaw function.
  • Cheekbone and Eye Socket Injuries: Fracture repair to restore symmetry and function to the face.
  • Soft Tissue Injuries: Suturing and reconstructive procedures for lacerations on the face, lips, and oral cavity.
Sports Injuries
  • Treatment for dental and facial trauma caused during sports activities.
Temporomandibular Joint (TMJ) Dislocations and Injuries
  • Reduction of dislocated jaws and management of TMJ dysfunction and pain.
Facial Burns and Chemical Injuries
  • Immediate care for burns or chemical exposure affecting the face and oral cavity.
Emergency Treatment for Oral Infections and Abscesses
  • Surgical drainage and infection control to prevent complications and spread to adjacent areas.
Pediatric Emergency Care
  • Management of dental trauma in children, including tooth reimplantation and space maintenance.
