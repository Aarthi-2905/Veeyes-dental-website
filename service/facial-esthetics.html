Esthetic Dentistry

  • Facial Esthetics in Dentistry refers to the dental procedures and treatments that enhance or restore the overall appearance of the face, with a special focus on the smile, lips, teeth, and jawline.
  • It blends cosmetic dentistry with elements of facial aesthetics to achieve a harmonious, youthful, and natural look that complements the patient's facial features.

• Aligning and whitening teeth.

• Rejuvenating perioral (around the mouth) tissues.

• Enhancing facial symmetry

• Restoring volume and correcting wrinkles.
