Implant dentistry

  • Implant dentistry offers a permanent, stable, and aesthetic solution for tooth replacement with artificial tooth roots made of biocompatible materials (typically titanium or zirconia). It improves oral function and quality of life, especially for patients who struggle with removable dentures or multiple missing teeth.
  • Implant procedures are used for single tooth or multiple teeth, full mouth rehabilitation, supporting removable dentures, and to improve the stability of toothless jaws.

• The implant is surgically inserted into the jawbone under local anesthesia or sedation.

• Healing time (osseointegration) is typically 3–6 months, during which the implant bonds with the bone.

• Immediate teeth in esthetic area and full arch teeth replacement

• Once healed, an abutment and crown/bridge are placed.

• Final restoration is custom-designed for function, comfort, and aesthetics.

Benefits of Dental Implants:

  • Natural look and feel
  • Preserves jawbone and facial structure
  • Improves chewing efficiency and speech
  • Long-lasting (often 10–20+ years with proper care)
  • No impact on neighboring teeth (unlike traditional bridges)

Digital implant planning

At Veeyes we use Digital implant planning to virtually plan the placement of dental implants with high precision and predictability. It combines 3D imaging (like CBCT scans) with digital intraoral scans to create a complete, virtual model of the patient’s mouth.
