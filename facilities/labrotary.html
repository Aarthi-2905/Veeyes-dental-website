Blood Investigation Laboratory

Veeyes Dental is equipped with a blood investigation laboratory for rapid and accurate diagnosis and managing patients especially those undergoing surgical or complex dental treatments like implants, extractions, or periodontal surgeries.

  • Laboratory pre-surgical Screening ensures the patient is fit for procedures like extractions, implant placement, or oral surgery.
  • Blood tests help to detect systemic disease links and monitor diseases that may affect oral health.
  • For patients with heart disease, diabetes, bleeding disorders, etc., lab results guide treatment planning

CADCAM Digital Dental Lab (4 axis & 5 axis) at Veeyes

  • Our CAD/CAM digital dental lab with 4-axis and 5-axis milling machines enables faster, more precise, and customized restorations across multiple dental specialties.
  • The 3D design and milling machines are used to fabricate crowns, bridges, veneers, and other restorations.
  • Using this advanced digital technology, we design and fabricate accurate and high- quality restorations that match the patients' natural teeth.
