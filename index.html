Kids and Family Dentistry

Kids Dentistry

The delicate baby teeth demand special care and attention, despite being temporary. Pediatric Dentistry, or Kids Dentistry, is the specialized field of dentistry dedicated to safeguarding the oral health of children from infancy through adolescence.

Family Dentistry

Why is Pediatric Dentist Important?

  • Catch Problems Early: Prevent bigger dental issues in the future.

  • Build Healthy Habits: Teach kids & parents the importance of oral hygiene for lifelong benefits.

  • Positive Experiences: Child-friendly care helps kids feel safe and relaxed.

5 Tips for Your Child’s Healthy Teeth

  • Brush teeth twice daily, morning and evening.

  • Avoid sleeping with milk bottles.

  • Use toothpaste with optimal fluoride.

  • Reduce sugary foods; encourage fruits, vegetables and non-sweetened yogurt.

  • Promote calcium-rich foods like milk, cheese, and fresh green vegetables.
