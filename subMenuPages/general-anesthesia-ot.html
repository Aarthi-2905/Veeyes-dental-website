Accident & Emergency Care

  • A (National Accreditation Board for Hospitals and Healthcare Providers)-accredited dental hospital is a healthcare facility that meets rigorous standards for patient care, safety, and quality.
  • These standards are set by the Council of India and ensure that the hospital provides high-quality services while maintaining a high level of patient safety and hygiene.

Operation Theatre (OT) in a NABH-accredited dental hospital must comply with specific guidelines to ensure that all surgical procedures, especially those related to dental care, are carried out in a safe, effective, and sterile environment.

Scope of Accident & Emergency Services:

  • Sterility and Infection Control
  • Adherence to Safety Protocols
  • Quality Assurance
  • Advanced Technology
  • Qualified and Trained Staff
  • Anesthesia and Pain Management
  • Post-Surgery Care
