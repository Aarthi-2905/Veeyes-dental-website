Discounts

  1. 1. Seasonal Promotions

    • Festive Offers: Celebrate festivals and special occasions with a healthy smile! Avail exciting discounts during major festivals like Diwali, Christmas, and Pongal.
    • School Holiday Promotions: Special packages for children and teens during school holidays to ensure healthy smiles year-round.

  2. 2. Referral Programs

    • Spread the word and save!
    • Refer a friend or family member and receive discounts on your next dental procedure.
    • Enjoy a free consultation for every successful referral.

  3. 3. Annual Health Program

    • Comprehensive dental care all year-round.
    • Includes routine cleanings, oral health check-ups, and preventive treatments.
    • Stay on top of your dental health with exclusive benefits and cost savings.

  4. 4. Family Dental Health Plan

    • Care for your entire family under one affordable plan.
    • Discounts on dental cleanings, cavity treatments, and gum care for all family members.
    • Tailored for families of all sizes to ensure smiles stay bright together.

  5. 5. Teen Dental Health Plan

    • Specialized care for growing smiles.
    • Focused on orthodontic check-ups, cavity prevention, and wisdom teeth monitoring.
    • Ideal for teenagers to build lifelong healthy dental habits.

  6. 6. White Teeth Annual Plan

    • Keep your smile radiant all year long.
    • Includes professional teeth whitening sessions, cleanings, and stain removal treatments.
    • Perfect for individuals seeking care for sparkling white teeth.

  7. 7. Social Esthetics Plan

    • Smile confidently in social and professional settings.
    • Customized treatments like teeth whitening, gum contouring, and veneers for a flawless smile.
    • Boosts confidence with an aesthetically pleasing smile tailored just for you.
