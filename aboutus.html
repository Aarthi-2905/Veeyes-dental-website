“20 Years of Trust”

VEEYES DENTAL HOSPITAL is India’s largest state-of-the-art dental facility, dedicated to delivering exceptional care. With over 20 years of excellence, VeeYes Dental Hospital remains a trusted name in dentistry. Guided by our motto, “Healthy Teeth, Healthy Lifestyle,” we strive to help every patient achieve optimal oral health.

As one of the first standalone dental hospitals in India to achieve NABH accreditation, we uphold the highest standards of safety and quality in dental care.

Our Journey of Excellence
2005

• In 2005, Dr. Ramesh Shanmugam founded Sanjeevi Dental Clinic in Tirupur, Tamil Nadu.

• This 600-square-foot clinic quickly became known for its expertise in dental and facial surgeries, adopting cutting-edge technologies.

2012

•In 2012, the practice expanded into VEEYES DENTAL CARE, with Dr. Ranjana Ramesh joining the team.

•A new branch was established in Coimbatore, growing into a 2,500-square-foot facility with six operatory rooms. Patients from across Tamil Nadu, including Erode, Ooty, and Salem, sought care here.

2022

In 2022, we expanded into a 35,000-square-foot, fully digitalized facility. Key features include:

  • 27 dental operatory rooms with digital X-rays
  • A surgical operation theatre with an ICU
  • 15 inpatient rooms for extended care
  • In-house CAD-CAM and blood investigation labs
  • Advanced learning center, pharmacy, and canteen services

Our advanced diagnostic tools, including Digital OPG, CBCT, and intraoral scanners, ensure precise diagnoses and effective treatments. The CSSD (Central Sterile Supply Department) department enforces strict sterilization protocols for unparalleled hygiene and safety.

2024

• In 2024, we were accredited by “National Accreditation Board for Hospitals & Healthcare Providers (NABH)”, reflecting our unwavering commitment to quality care, patient safety, and clinical excellence.

• Our accreditation by NABH is a testament to our dedication to excellence in clinical services, infection control, and ethical practice.

Today, VEEYES DENTAL HOSPITAL serves patients from across the state and internationally, providing advanced treatments for facial pain, oral complications, and long-term dental health.

Specialized Care

Under the leadership of Dr. Ramesh Shanmugam and Dr. Ranjana Ramesh, our expert team provides advanced treatments, such as:

  • Full-mouth rehabilitation with a multidisciplinary approach
  • Smile makeovers for aesthetic perfection
  • Corrective jaw surgeries for functional and facial improvements

With a combination of expertise and cutting-edge technology, we ensure comfortable, high-quality care for every patient.

Community Outreach

As part of our commitment to improving oral health, we organize dental camps across Coimbatore and Tirupur using a mobile dental van. These initiatives provide quality care to underserved communities while raising awareness about oral hygiene.

Founders

Ramesh Shanmugam, M.D

Dr. Ramesh, a distinguished dental surgeon, has made substantial contributions to the fields of Orthodontics and Oral Implantology. With a rich background encompassing twenty-two years of experience in clinical, academic, research, and administrative roles, he has played a pivotal role in advancing dental care.

His academic journey includes the attainment of a master’s degree in Orthodontics and Dentofacial Orthopaedics, showcasing his commitment to specialized education. Dr. Ramesh holds the prestigious title of Honorary Diplomat from the Indian Board of Orthodontics and has undergone extensive training and certification in various dental disciplines from reputable institutes worldwide. Additionally, he is a certified Oral Implantologist from Manipal University.

Ranjana Ramesh, Ph.D. BDS, MDS

Dental Surgeon, Maxilla Facial Prosthodontics & BPS Specialist

Dr. Ranjana is a distinguished dental professional with both her Bachelor’s and Master’s degrees in Dental Surgery from Dr. MGR Medical University, Tamilnadu. A Gold Medalist in Oral Anatomy and Histology, she was also honored with the prestigious International PFA Senior Student Award by the Pierre Fauchard Academy — a testament to her academic excellence and dedication to dentistry.

With over 22 years of experience, Dr. Ranjana is a highly sought-after cosmetic dentist, dental surgeon, and certified BPS (Bio functional Prosthetic System) specialist. She currently practices across Chennai, Coimbatore, and Tirupur, offering advanced dental care to patients from India and abroad. Alongside Dr. Ramesh, she co-manages two large, state-of-the-art dental specialty centers in Coimbatore and Tirupur, delivering comprehensive oral health services to patients of all age.

Dr. Ranjana’s areas of specialization include:
  • Cosmetic Dentistry
  • BPS Prosthodontics
  • Full Mouth Rehabilitation
  • Laser Dentistry
  • Implant Prosthetic Dentistry

Renowned for her warm demeanor and precision in complex dental treatments, Dr. Ranjana excels in treating elderly patients and those with advanced dental needs. Her ability to quickly build trust and ensure patient comfort has earned her a loyal following.

In addition to her clinical work, Dr. Ranjana actively contributes to ongoing dental research and stays at the forefront of emerging trends in modern dentistry. Her approach goes beyond treatment — she advocates for holistic oral health and preventive care, helping her patients maintain long-term wellness.
